<Note>Action trace only supported for iOS. Android in development.</Note>

To see camelQA's actions, test durations, and screenshots, follow these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Report Navigator">
    After completing a test, locate the Report Navigator at the top of the left side menu.
    <Frame>
      <img width="600" src="/images/debug-ios-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the test you would like to see the action trace for">
    <Frame>
      <img width="600" src="/images/debug-ios-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the test, observe the results">
    This view allows you to see each action taken and screenshot evaluated for assertCondition and waitUntil.
    <Frame>
      <img width="600" src="/images/debug-ios-3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
